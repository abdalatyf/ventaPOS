# Product Guidelines

## Language and Localization
- The entire application MUST be in **Arabic** and Right-to-Left (**RTL**).
- The tone must be "لغة سوق مهنية ومبسطة" (professional but easy Egyptian Arabic). 
- Avoid strict corporate Arabic and excessive street slang.
- Key terms: (العميل, البضاعة, الخزنة, الفاتورة, الدفتر, النقدية).

## UI/UX Rules
- Dynamic, premium, rich aesthetics.
- Use glassmorphism, subtle micro-animations, and smooth gradients.
- Must feel state-of-the-art and "wow" the user on first glance.
